Audience

Here at weather geared, we are looking to help those that want to know what to wear for the day. If its sunny but -19° outside, we got a image recommendation for that.

What if it's cloudy but 86° outside? Wear shorts/skirt, and a short-sleeve T-shirt. We got a recommendation for any day of the week, any weather forcast, there there's something you can wear.

persona 1

Indecisive

Someone who might not know what to wear for that day. They have an outdoors appointment and its going to rain an hour into their meeting, what do you wear? Thats what they will use this site for. You check the weather, and get an instant recommendation.

persona 2

Parents

Parents want to get moving they have places to be, people to see and want to know what to wear for themselves and for the children, this site will help you make those quick decisions. If it's cold out, grab a hoodie, is it somewhere in the middle, throw on a couple layers.

Scenarios

  1. Persona 1 wanted to know when the next storm is and what to wear, its starting out cold with a rain but ends with a snow storm, be prepared with what to wear.
  2. Persona 2 was thinking that today was sunny and not to cold, they checked and its raining later, so a couple layers will help.
  3. Persona 1 has been looking for the best day to hike up the mountains with their friends but want the right amount of layers.
  4. Persona 2 is setting up a birthday party later and need to keep their children warm, so they check for what to wear that day.
  5. Persona 1 was going on a hunting trip, brought a jacket and saw that later there is a snow storm hitting, so they know to bring some real weather gear.
